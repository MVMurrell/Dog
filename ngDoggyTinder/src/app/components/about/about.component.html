About


Welcome to Doggle! Doggle is a matching service for people who want to find dogs for their dogs to play with, and in the process become friends or possibly even more with their owners as well!


We created this website as our final project for Skill Distillery's 16 week Java Web Development course. Please visit our github repository for more information on our project: Repository
